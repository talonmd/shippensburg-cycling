The Super Swell Spring Century

New for 2018: The Waggoner's Gap Birding Edition

Lovely rural roads? Check.

Babbling brooks? Check.

Forest climbing? Check.

It's the 2018 Super Swell Spring Century, Waggoner's Gap Birding Edition, brought to you by the Shippensburg University Cycling Club.

Open to everyone.
No registration.
No fees.
No support.

100 mile (6,500' elevation gain) and 100 kilometer options (4,200' elevation gain).

And a lovely climb up Waggoner's Gap.

Links to maps and GPS tracks here (FULL CENTURY) and here (METRIC CENTRY).

If you have a Garmin, please download the appropriate file and load the ride. It's helpful to have multiple direction-spotters in each group. Because, you know, this is an UNSUPPORTED ride. You're on you're own. We're not going to purposely leave anyone stranded, but you are still you're own responsibility.

Date: Sunday, April 29th, 2015

Start Time: 8:00 AM

Start Location: Shippensburg Township Park

Hope you can join us! Send a note to nathan.goates@gmail.com if we should be expecting you, or check in on our Facebook page.
