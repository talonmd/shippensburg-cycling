Training Schedule and Other Team Events

Starlight Circle Races (Wednesdays)

Season: Early fall

Time: In 2019, 9:00 pm (lights required; if you have extra, please bring to share!)

Location: Meet at James Burd Elementary in Shippensburg

Description: A 30-40 minute criterium-style practice race. The course is a 0.9 mile loop around the parking lot / drop-off areas of James Burd Elementary and the Shippensburg Area Intermediate School.

Zero traffic. Spectator friendly. Lights required.

Points awarded based on weekly placing. The overall winner gets to say they're the overall winner. It's pretty awesome to win. Winner, winner, chicken dinner. Everyone wants to be a winner.
