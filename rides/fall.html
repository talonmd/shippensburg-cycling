The Freewheeling Fall Foliage 100 & The Friendly 5-Hour Century

Freewheeling Fall Foliage 100

Pay to ride 100 miles with a group? Not with us you won't. It's a fall festivus...er...century for the rest of us.

When: Sunday, October 7, 8:00 AM

Where: Meet at Shippensburg Township Park, just out of town on Brittan Rd. There are bathrooms. MAP

What: This is a RELAXED, CASUAL-PACED 100-mile or 100-kilometer ride. It is ABSOLUTELY FREE. But it is also unsupported.

Maps/GPS tracks: 100-MILE, 100-KILOMETER

Notes:
100-kilometer and 100-mile routes stay together until ICE CREAM at the 50-mile mark.
First water stop is at 50 miles, so please prepare appropriately.
For 100-mile riders, additional stops available at mile 60 (store), mile 65 (park facet) and mile 82 (gas station).

Friendly 5-Hour Century

ONE HUNDRED MILES IN FIVE HOURS. Well, sub-five hours, hopefully.

Anyway, what we do here is work together to get as many people as possible to finish a century under vie hours. Great success last year, so we’re doing it again.

Details for 2017:

  • Saturday, October 7. Depart 8:00 AM sharp. Rider meeting at 7:50.
  • Flat-ish route (some rollers, but no big deal)
  • Ride a 22-mph paceline, adjusting for hills
  • All time counts – stops, mechanicals, etc.
  • One scheduled stop after 65-miles - at the start area. You can leave bottles, food, etc. in your car and re-load for the last 35 miles.
  • Options to ride just the 65-mile loop (8:00 AM) or the later 35-mile loop (approx 11:00 AM)
  • Friendly! So, not racing or showing off your late-season top-end...just all working together. If you fall off the group once or twice, we’ll try to slow up for you…but if you just can’t maintain the pace, you’ll need to get back on your own.
  • Start address: 380 Britton Road, Shippensburg, PA 17257 (Shippensburg Township Park –parking & bathrooms.).
  • And…it’s all FREE!!!
